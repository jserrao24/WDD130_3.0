Q:What is the most important piece of equipment for river rafting?

The best equipment for river rafting is a good quality, well-fitting lifejacket. A life jacket can save you from drowning if you fall into the water or are swept away by strong currents, and it will also keep you afloat in case of an accident. You should wear two layers of clothing when river rafting: one layer on top (a light windproof jacket) and another layer underneath (a waterproof/breathable jacket). Wear gloves, long pants and closed shoes to protect yourself against sharp rocks and thorns that may be found along the riversides.

Q: Why the right river rafting equipment is important for safety

There are many dangers associated with river rafting, including but not limited to: -Injuries from falls -Injuries from collisions with other rafts or rocks -Injuries from getting lost -Injuries from getting caught in rapids -Injuries from getting swept away -Injuries from getting hypothermia. River rafting equipment can help keep rafters safe by providing them with a secure platform to stand on while rafting and by providing them with a means of communication and navigation.
